# Customer Churn Prediction | End-to-End Data Science Project

## Project Overview

Customer churn prediction is an important business problem that helps organizations identify customers who are likely to discontinue their services. This project presents an end-to-end Data Science solution that leverages Machine Learning to predict customer churn and Power BI to visualize business insights through an interactive dashboard.

The project demonstrates the complete data science lifecycle, including data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), feature engineering, machine learning model development, model evaluation, and business intelligence visualization.

---

## Problem Statement

Customer retention is critical for business growth. Predicting customer churn enables organizations to identify at-risk customers and take proactive measures to improve customer satisfaction and reduce revenue loss.

---

## Objectives

- Clean and preprocess customer data.
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Build and evaluate multiple Machine Learning models.
- Predict customer churn using the best-performing model.
- Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard for business insights.
- Support data-driven decision-making through visualization.

---

## Dataset

## Key Business Insights

- Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** are more likely to churn.
- Customers using **Fiber Optic Internet Service** exhibit higher churn behaviour.
- Customers paying through **Electronic Check** show a higher likelihood of churn.
- Customers with longer tenure generally demonstrate stronger customer retention.
- Contract type and internet service are significant factors influencing churn prediction.
